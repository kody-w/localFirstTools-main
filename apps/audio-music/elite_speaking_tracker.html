Elite Speaking Tracker

Track clarity, texture, and comfort based on the "How To Speak Brilliantly" framework.

Clarity (Clear Thinking → Clear Speaking)

  • Identify 10 topics you want clarity on
  • Record a 3-minute video daily on one topic
  • Repeat topics for 20 days total
  • Reduce low-quality inputs (content, noise, distraction)

Texture (Word Choice & Vivid Language)

  • Collect articulate phrases & metaphors
  • Practice TAKES (Time, Audio, Kinesthetic, Eyes, Smell)
  • Rewrite dull phrases with sharper or surprising language
  • Repeat improved phrases aloud

Comfort (Body, Mind, Spirit)

Body

  • Diaphragmatic breathing
  • Box breathing (4x4x4x4)
  • Posture & voice warm-up

Mind

  • Define your mission
  • Mental primer: “I am here to serve”

Spirit

  • Define core values
  • Speak in alignment with beliefs
