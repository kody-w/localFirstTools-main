How to Play

Welcome to Gravity Golf Pro! Your goal is to get the glowing ball into the hole using gravity wells and physics.

PHASE 1: Place Gravity Wells
Click on the course to place up to 3 gravity wells
Adjust mass with the slider or mouse wheel
Wells pull the ball with inverse square law physics
PHASE 2: Launch the Ball
Click and drag from the ball to aim
Longer drag = more power
Release to launch!
Controls
